Road Race

Game Rules:

  1. Objective: The objective of the game is to maneuver the bike through the traffic and earn the highest score possible without colliding with other car and bikes.
  2. Starting the Game: To start the game, click on the "Start Game" button on the start screen.
  3. Controls: Use the arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to control the movement of the bike.
  4. Scoring: The player earns points continuously as the game progresses. The score is displayed on the screen. Try to achieve the highest score possible.
  5. Collision: If the bike collides with any other car and bike on the road, the game will end immediately.
  6. Restart: To restart the game and reset the score, click on the "Reset Highest Score" button on the start screen.
  7. Game Over: When the game ends due to a collision, the player can restart the game by clicking on the "Start Game" button again.
